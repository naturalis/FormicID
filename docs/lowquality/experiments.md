### Settings

Settings used when training the above dataset:

- `Augmentation`: `small`
- `num_epochs`: `10`
- `learning_rate`: `0.001`
- `batch_size`: `64`
- `dropout`: `0.5`
- `optimizer`: `Nadam`
- `model`: `InceptionV3`
- `seed`: `1`
- `testsplit`: `0.1`
- `validationsplit` : `0.2`
